● Here are the highest-impact improvements, grouped by category, based on what's
  currently in the codebase:

  ---
  Feature Engineering

  Currently: OHLCV + EMA (5 periods) + MACD + RSI (3 periods) = 21 features

  Add:

  1. VWAP + deviation bands — intraday price anchoring signal, more meaningful
  than pure EMA for short-horizon trades
  2. ATR (Average True Range) — you use a fixed height=1% barrier; ATR as a
  feature lets the model learn current volatility context, separate from the
  volatility-scaled labeling
  3. Bollinger Band %B and bandwidth — positions price within its distribution;
  bandwidth signals volatility expansion/contraction
  4. Rate of Change (ROC) at 5/10/20 bars — raw momentum, distinct from RSI's
  bounded version
  5. Time-of-day encoding (sin/cos) — include_time_features=true is in your
  config but verify it's producing cyclical encoding, not a raw integer.
  Intraday patterns (open/close surges) are highly time-dependent

  ---
  Labeling & Training Signal

  Currently: Triple-Barrier with gap_days=0

  Critical fix:

  6. Add a purging gap — gap_days=0 means overlapping labels between train and
  val/test. Since your width_minutes=40 means each label looks 40 minutes
  forward, any sample within 40 minutes of the boundary leaks future data. Set
  gap_days=1 (or at minimum a 40-minute gap)
  7. Meta-labeling (López de Prado ch. 4) — train a secondary classifier on top
  of primary model predictions to predict whether the primary model is correct.
  This turns 3-class into a confidence filter and can dramatically improve
  actual P&L by abstaining on low-confidence calls
  8. Sample uniqueness weighting — CUSUM bars have overlapping label windows;
  weight each sample by 1 / (number of concurrent labels) to reduce overfit on
  clustered events

  ---
  Model Architecture

  Currently: No attention, no Transformer

  9. Temporal Fusion Transformer (TFT) or a lightweight self-attention block on
  top of your LSTM — your TSB/ResNLS models treat the sequence as a fixed-length
  input; attention lets the model weight which timesteps matter most per
  prediction
  10. Separate buy/sell heads — instead of a single 3-class head, use two binary
  heads (buy vs. not-buy, sell vs. not-sell). Trains more naturally since the
  asymmetry between long and short opportunities differs

  ---
  Regime Filtering

  11. Market regime feature — add a 1-minute VIX or SPY correlation feature per
  ticker. The model currently trains blind to whether it's a
  trending/ranging/volatile regime; conditioning on regime significantly reduces
  false signals
  12. Time-of-week dummies — Monday opens and Friday closes behave very
  differently; a simple sin/cos week encoding adds almost-free signal

  ---
  Training Process

  Currently: Adam + fixed LR + early stopping on val_accuracy

  13. Cosine annealing LR schedule — your current setup uses a flat lr=1e-3
  until early stopping. Cosine decay prevents the model from getting stuck near
  a plateau at the end of training
  14. Focal loss instead of class-balanced CE — focal loss down-weights easy
  (correctly-classified) examples dynamically per batch, more adaptive than
  static inverse-frequency weights
  15. Time-series augmentation — add small Gaussian noise to features during
  training (jitter) and random magnitude scaling (0.9–1.1×). Regularizes without
  changing the temporal structure
