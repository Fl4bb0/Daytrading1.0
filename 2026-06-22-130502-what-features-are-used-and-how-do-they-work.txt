  StandardizedFeatures вҖ” wraps any of the above and z-scores them

  2. Meta-model features вҖ” src/kvant/meta/features.py

  These sit on top of the base classifier's predictions (not on price data) and
  are used to rank/filter trades rather than predict direction:

  - side вҖ” Вұ1 from the predicted label (SHORT=0 вҶ’ -1, BUY=2 вҶ’ +1)
  - side_confidence вҖ” the model's predicted probability for whichever side it
  picked (prob_SHORT or prob_BUY)
  - ticker_side_prior_score вҖ” a causal, shrinkage-smoothed historical mean PnL
  for that (ticker, side) pair: (local_sum + kВ·global_mean) / (local_n + k),
  blending the ticker-specific track record toward the global average when
  there's little local history
  - ticker_side_prior_n вҖ” how many historical trades back that prior

  The causal part is important: a heap (pending) holds completed trades keyed by
  bar_close_time and only folds them into the running sums once their close
  time is вүӨ the current row's entry timestamp вҖ” so the prior never uses
  information from a trade that hadn't closed yet (no lookahead).
